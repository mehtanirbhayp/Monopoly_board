üö® Quarantine & System Failure System

üè• Quarantine (Jail) System

When a player lands on the Quarantine space (index 10), they are placed in isolation for safety reasons.

  • Immediate Options: Pay $50 to get out immediately OR lose one turn
  • Visual Effects: Special jail transition animation with red border
  • Status Tracking: Player's "inJail" status is tracked in the system
  • Manual Resolution: Player must manually choose their action through notification buttons

‚ö° System Failure (Go to Jail)

When a player lands on the System Failure space (index 30), they encounter a critical system malfunction.

  • Dramatic Animation: 2-second jail transition animation with rotation and color effects
  • Automatic Transport: Player is automatically moved to Quarantine (space 10)
  • Special Notification: "System Failure!" error notification with red styling
  • Quarantine Rules Apply: Same $50 payment or lose turn options as regular Quarantine
  • Visual Feedback: Special jail notification styling with dark red background

üîÑ Quarantine Mechanics

  • Payment Option: Pay $50 to immediately exit quarantine and continue playing
  • Wait Option: Lose one turn and remain in quarantine until next turn
  • Insufficient Funds: If player can't afford $50, they must wait one turn
  • Status Updates: Game log records all quarantine actions and releases

üéØ How to Play

  1. Setup: Use the hamburger menu to add 2-8 players and configure their tokens
  2. Start Game: Click "Start Game" to begin the first turn
  3. Movement: Choose manual move, dice roll, or teleport to move your token
  4. Property Actions: Buy unowned properties or pay rent to owners
  5. Special Spaces: Handle chance cards, community chest, taxes, and utilities
  6. Quarantine: If sent to quarantine, choose to pay $50 or lose a turn
  7. End Turn: Complete your actions and pass to the next player

üèÜ Strategy Tips

  • Color Groups: Try to acquire complete color groups for maximum rent potential
  • Transit Routes: Transit routes provide steady income and are valuable investments
  • Utilities: While variable, utilities can provide good returns in the right circumstances
  • Quarantine Management: Keep $50 in reserve for emergency quarantine exits
  • Property Priority: Focus on higher-rent properties in the later game stages
  • Bank Management: Monitor your finances carefully to avoid bankruptcy
